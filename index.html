About

We made this app to quantify the amount of misogynistic language used by individual hip hop artists. Type in the name of an MC and we’ll assess their lyrics based on what’s on Rapgenius.com. Having crunched the propotion of misogynistic language (including terms like bitch, ho, etc), we’ll report it back to you in an easy-to-read score.

It’s not an acid test, but it should provide a rough indicator of what to expect when you’re listening to a new artist. We made it because there’s an overwhelming quantity of sexist language in hip hop, and it can be disappointing to find out that a newly discovered gem is actually a misogynistic fool. Now you can use this app to spot the sexist MCs in advance. We hope you find it useful.

Made by Anders Fisher and Sarah Fisher.
